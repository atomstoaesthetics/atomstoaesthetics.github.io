He Asked, She Said Yes!


We are Utsav and Sanjana, and our journey began in the bustling hallways of our school when we were just 11 years old. Utsav, with his mischievous grin and playful pranks, couldn't help but tease Sanjana, drawn to her infectious laughter and radiant smile that lit up every room. Even then, he recognized something special in her. Sanjana, with her kind heart and easygoing nature, was always a joy to be around. Her genuine warmth and openness made her approachable, and her ability to find humor in any situation was incredibly endearing. Little did they know that their youthful interactions were laying the foundation for a deeper connection. After school, life's currents carried us in different directions, but thanks to the wonders of social media, we never truly lost touch. We watched each other's lives unfold through Instagram, sharing milestones and experiences from afar. Then, in early 2022, a simple message rekindled our connection. A casual conversation blossomed into hours-long chats, as we discovered a remarkable alignment in our thoughts, values, and dreams for the future. It was as if the years apart had only strengthened the bond that began in our childhood. Utsav, eager to see Sanjana again, made frequent trips to Toronto, where their first official date at the elegant Canoe restaurant solidified their feelings for each other. With each monthly visit, their love story unfolded, leading them on countless adventures together â€“ from exploring bustling city streets to relaxing on tranquil beaches. In just over a year, their love blossomed into an engagement in May 2023, a joyous occasion filled with laughter, tears, and the heartwarming union of their families in India. For Utsav, Sanjana's smile is not just a physical feature but a reflection of her beautiful soul. Her humility, a rare and precious trait, is the cornerstone of their relationship, fostering a deep understanding and unwavering support for each other. Her ability to transform his mood with a single smile is a testament to the depth of their connection and the power of their love. From that very first date to their engagement, their path towards marriage was clear and undeniable. And now, the time has come for us to celebrate our love with all of you, our dearest family and friends.

" When you realize you want to spend the rest of your life with somebody, you want the rest of your life to start as soon as possible! "
- Utsav to Sanjana
