He Asked, She Said Yes!

" When you realize you want to spend the rest of your life with somebody, you want the rest of your life to start as soon as possible! "

Hi everyone! We're Utsav and Sanjana, and we're so excited to share our story with you. Believe it or not, we've known each other since we were just 11 years old, back in our school days. Utsav, being the mischievous one, used to irritate Sanjana quite a bit. But there was something about her infectious smile and bubbly personality that he found irresistible. Even though we lost touch for a while after school, social media kept us loosely connected. It wasn't until early 2022 that a casual conversation rekindled our friendship. Soon, we were chatting day and night, discovering how much we had in common. It felt like fate had brought us back together. Utsav finally admitted that his childhood teasing was just a way to get Sanjana's attention! Our journey as a couple began at Canoe restaurant in Toronto, where we shared a memorable evening taking in the breathtaking views of the CN Tower. This marked the start of countless adventures, each one strengthening our bond. Utsav frequently visited Toronto while he was completing his PhD, and Sanjana treasured every opportunity to spend time with Utsav as we knew we found something special. We shared the news with our families and got engaged in May 2023 during a beautiful trip to India, where our parents met for the first time. For Utsav, Sanjana's radiant smile and genuine humility are what make her truly remarkable. Her smile has the power to lift his spirits, and her humility is the foundation of their strong partnership. As a couple, we complement each other beautifully. Utsav appreciates the scientific method and logical reasoning, while Sanjana has a strong intuition for creativity. This combination of qualities makes us better people and enriches our lives together. From that first date to our engagement, getting married was always on the horizon, and now, that time has finally come!


After saying "I do" at Toronto City Hall, we treated ourselves and our closest friends to a delicious vegan lunch at Planta Queen's in downtown Toronto. It was the perfect way to celebrate our love and kick off our new life together surrounded by amazing food and even better company.
