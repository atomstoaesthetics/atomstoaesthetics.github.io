Getting to Mana Resort

Mana Resort is easily accessible by air, train, or road:

  • By Air: The closest airport is Maharana Pratap Airport in Udaipur (UDR), approximately 110 km away. From there, you can hire a taxi or arrange for a private transfer to the resort.

  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Falna | Distance 30 km.

  • By Road: Mana Resort is well-connected by road to major cities in Rajasthan. If you're driving, GPS navigation can easily guide you to the resort.

Things to See and Do Nearby

Ranakpur is famous for its stunning Jain temples, particularly the intricately carved Ranakpur Jain Temple. Other attractions in the area include:

  • Kumbhalgarh Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its massive walls and panoramic views.
  • Sadri Village: A charming village where you can witness traditional Rajasthani life.
  • Jawai Leopard Camp: For the adventurous, explore the wilderness and spot leopards in their natural habitat.
