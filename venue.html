Mana Resort

| Ranakpur, India |


We have fallen in love with the magical Mana Resort in Ranakpur, a hidden gem nestled amidst the Aravalli hills. The serene setting speaks to our love for nature and peace, making it the perfect backdrop for our wedding. One of us feels a deep connection to the resort's traditional Rajasthani architecture, a beautiful echo of their heritage, while the other finds its tranquil ambiance a true reflection of their spirit.

We can't wait to tie the knot in the sprawling gardens, under the canopy of a centuries-old banyan tree, surrounded by vibrant blooms and the starry night sky. The gentle murmur of the nearby stream and the sweet melodies of birdsong will create an atmosphere of pure magic. Mana Resort, with its unique blend of old-world charm and modern luxury, promises to make our wedding an unforgettable celebration of love, set against the breathtaking beauty of Ranakpur. The region is renowned for its stunning Jain temples, particularly the intricately carved Ranakpur Jain Temple, a marvel of architecture and spirituality. We encourage our guests to explore this sacred site and immerse themselves in its rich history and cultural significance.

For those seeking adventure and natural beauty, Ranakpur offers a wealth of attractions beyond the temple. Embark on a day trip to the majestic Kumbhalgarh Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its massive walls and panoramic views. Wander through the charming Sadri village, where you can witness traditional Rajasthani life and interact with the friendly locals. And for the truly adventurous, venture into the wilderness at Jawai Leopard Camp, where you might catch a glimpse of these elusive creatures in their natural habitat.

Getting to Mana Resort ...

[Address: Plot #4677, Ranakpur road, Sadri, Rajasthan 306702, India]
Mana Resort is easily accessible by air, train, or road:

  • By Air: The closest airport is Maharana Pratap Airport in Udaipur (UDR), approximately 110 km away. From there, you can hire a taxi or arrange for a private transfer to the resort.

  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Falna, about 30 km from the resort. Taxis and local transportation options are available from the station.

  • By Road: Mana Resort is well-connected by road to major cities in Rajasthan. If you're driving, GPS navigation can easily guide you to the resort.
